Many months pass and things only get worse. Students are now all online doing virtual schooling. Social interaction barely exists now. People lose their jobs due to the now-global pandemic. This causes a global economic recession. Trump almost starts World War III by killing an Iranian General with a drone. He is impeached, but not removed from office. Kobe Bryant and his daughter die in a helicopter crash. A plane crash in Tehran kills 176 people. Prince Harry and Megan Markle renounce their royalty. The UK leaves the EU. A white cop kneels on George Floyd’s neck for nine minutes, on camera. He dies, beginning a revolution for rights called Black Lives Matter. Wildfires in the west cause the sun to turn red. Murder hornets storm the Pacific Northwest. Puerto Rico is hit with many earthquakes. Flint, Michigan doesn’t have any clean water. Locust swarms are devastating Africa and Asia. A Saharan dust storm sweeps over southern US. A dam bursts in Michigan and floods a bunch of towns.
